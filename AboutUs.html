ABOUT US

Xtreme Sports Studio Pvt. Ltd. is owned by Mr. Shrikant Bapat, an Entrepreneur.After establishing sports studio,he decided also to go with sports business.

It was his dream to do something which will be beneficial to the local people of villages.It is a 30000 sq. ft plot having Lawn Tennis Court, Mini-Cricket & Football Grounds, Basketball & Volleyball Courts, Golf Driving Ranges and Cycling ,table tennis,snooker,caram,chess&Running tracks. In addition to this, there are 2 cricketing nets also (with bowling machine in 1 net). The main aim behind this idea is to make all the sports facilities available to all the sportspeople under one roof.
